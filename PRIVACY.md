# Emberleap Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: April 25, 2026**

Emberleap ("the App") is developed by Raw Plus Dry LLC. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

Emberleap does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. The App runs entirely on your device.

## What the App Does

- **Web Browsing**: Emberleap includes a built-in web browser. Web pages you visit are loaded directly from their servers to your device. We do not track or log your browsing activity.
- **Local History**: Your browsing and action history is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device.
- **Obsidian Integration**: If you connect an Obsidian vault, the App writes files directly to your local vault folder. No data is sent to any server.
- **Third-Party Services**: If you configure optional integrations (such as ShelfRead), data is sent directly from your device to those services. We do not act as an intermediary.

## Data Storage

All app settings and history are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. No data is synced to any cloud service by the App.

## Third-Party Content

The App displays web content from third-party websites. These websites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of websites you visit through the App.

## Children's Privacy

The App is rated 16+ due to unrestricted web access and is not intended for children under 16.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page.
